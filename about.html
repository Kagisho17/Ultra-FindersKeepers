About FindersKeepers

FindersKeepers is a dedicated lost-and-found platform for students and guests at our institution. Our mission is to help people quickly report, search, and claim lost items in a safe and easy way.

Users can report lost or found items, browse recently reported items, and claim belongings. Our platform ensures items are reunited with their owners efficiently.

We value trust, transparency, and convenience. With a modern design, search functionality, and responsive layout, FindersKeepers makes finding lost items effortless.
